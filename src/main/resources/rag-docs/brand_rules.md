# Brand to Occasion Rules

## Luxury Jewellery Brands
Brands: Tiffany and Co, Cartier, Pandora, Swarovski, Kay Jewellers, Zales, Signet, Brilliant Earth.
These brands are strongly associated with ANNIVERSARY (milestone year gifts), VALENTINES_DAY (romantic jewellery in January-February), BIRTHDAY (personalised or milestone pieces), and MOTHERS_DAY (sentimental keepsakes like lockets and birthstone jewellery). Jewellery from luxury brands elevates confidence for romantic or milestone occasions.

Primary occasions: ANNIVERSARY, VALENTINES_DAY. Secondary: BIRTHDAY, MOTHERS_DAY.
Category mapping: Jewellery and Accessories.

## Flowers and Gifting Delivery Brands
Brands: 1-800-Flowers, Teleflora, FTD, Bloom and Wild, Interflora, Bouqs, UrbanStems, ProFlowers.
These brands peak in February (VALENTINES_DAY) and May (MOTHERS_DAY). Year-round, they signal BIRTHDAY. Orders from these brands in January-February strongly indicate VALENTINES_DAY; in April-May, they indicate MOTHERS_DAY.

Primary occasions: VALENTINES_DAY (Jan-Feb), MOTHERS_DAY (Apr-May). Secondary: BIRTHDAY.
Category mapping: Flowers and Plants.

## Chocolate and Confectionery Brands
Brands: Godiva, Lindt, Ferrero Rocher, Sees Candies, Hotel Chocolat, Thorntons, Neuhaus, Vosges.
Heart-shaped or boxed chocolates in February strongly indicate VALENTINES_DAY. Festive collections in November-December indicate CHRISTMAS. Personalised selections indicate BIRTHDAY.

Primary occasions: VALENTINES_DAY (Feb), CHRISTMAS (Nov-Dec). Secondary: BIRTHDAY.
Category mapping: Chocolates and Confectionery.

## Consumer Electronics Brands
Brands: Apple, Samsung, Sony, Bose, JBL, Garmin, Fitbit, GoPro, Dyson, Sonos.
Electronics are popular for CHRISTMAS (November-December orders), BIRTHDAY (milestone gifts for teens and adults), and FATHERS_DAY (gadgets for dads in May-June).

Primary occasions: CHRISTMAS (Nov-Dec), BIRTHDAY. Secondary: FATHERS_DAY.
Category mapping: Electronics and Gadgets.

## Luxury Fashion and Fragrance Brands
Brands: Chanel, Dior, Gucci, Louis Vuitton, Versace, YSL, Tom Ford, Jo Malone, Burberry, Hermes.
Fragrances and luxury accessories from these brands are popular for BIRTHDAY (milestone luxury gifts), ANNIVERSARY (premium couple gifts), VALENTINES_DAY (romantic fragrances), and CHRISTMAS (premium gift selections). February orders suggest VALENTINES_DAY; June orders for partners suggest ANNIVERSARY.

Primary occasions: BIRTHDAY, ANNIVERSARY, VALENTINES_DAY. Secondary: CHRISTMAS.
Category mapping: Fashion and Fragrance.

## Spa and Wellness Brands
Brands: Lush, The Body Shop, Elemis, Rituals, Bath and Body Works, LOccitane, Molton Brown, Diptyque.
Spa and wellness products are strongly associated with MOTHERS_DAY (pampering gift sets in April-May), BIRTHDAY (self-care gifts year-round), VALENTINES_DAY (romantic bath sets), and CHRISTMAS (festive gift sets in November-December).

Primary occasions: MOTHERS_DAY (Apr-May), BIRTHDAY. Secondary: VALENTINES_DAY, CHRISTMAS.
Category mapping: Spa and Wellness.

## Premium Food and Drink Brands
Brands: Fortnum and Mason, Harrods, Harry and David, Wolfermans, Moet and Chandon, Dom Perignon, Godiva, Williams Sonoma.
Premium hampers and food gifts peak at CHRISTMAS (festive hampers), THANKSGIVING (harvest baskets in November), ANNIVERSARY (fine wine and champagne for couples), and BIRTHDAY (luxury food gifts year-round).

Primary occasions: CHRISTMAS (Nov-Dec), THANKSGIVING (Nov). Secondary: ANNIVERSARY, BIRTHDAY.
Category mapping: Food Hampers and Drink.

## Toy and Gaming Brands
Brands: LEGO, Mattel, Hasbro, Nintendo, Xbox, PlayStation, Funko Pop, Hot Wheels, Fisher-Price.
Toys and games are most strongly associated with CHRISTMAS (peak gifting category in November-December) and BIRTHDAY (gifts for children and gaming enthusiasts year-round). Child or sibling relation combined with a toy product and November-December date gives very high confidence for CHRISTMAS.

Primary occasions: CHRISTMAS (Nov-Dec), BIRTHDAY. Category mapping: Toys and Games.
