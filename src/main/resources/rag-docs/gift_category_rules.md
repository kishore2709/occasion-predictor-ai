# Gift Category to Occasion Rules

## Jewellery and Accessories
Rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings, watches, and personalised accessories are strongly associated with ANNIVERSARY as classic milestone gifts (1st, 5th, 10th, 25th, 50th year). Heart-shaped or romantic pieces point to VALENTINES_DAY. Birthstone or engraved items suggest BIRTHDAY, especially milestone birthdays. Lockets and sentimental keepsakes commonly appear for MOTHERS_DAY.

Confidence boost: jewellery product + romantic relation → VALENTINES_DAY or ANNIVERSARY. Jewellery + maternal relation + May order → MOTHERS_DAY.

## Flowers and Plants
Bouquets, floral arrangements, plants, and potted flowers are closely associated with VALENTINES_DAY (red roses in January-February), MOTHERS_DAY (lilies, tulips, mixed bouquets in April-May), BIRTHDAY (bright, colourful arrangements year-round), and ANNIVERSARY (romantic rose arrangements).

Confidence boost: flowers + order date February 1-14 → VALENTINES_DAY. Flowers + maternal relation + May → MOTHERS_DAY.

## Chocolates and Confectionery
Chocolates, truffles, candy boxes, and confectionery sets are strongly associated with VALENTINES_DAY (heart-shaped boxes), CHRISTMAS (festive selections and advent calendars), and BIRTHDAY (personalised chocolate gifts). Gourmet food sets around November point to THANKSGIVING.

Confidence boost: heart-shaped chocolate + February → VALENTINES_DAY.

## Electronics and Gadgets
Devices, headphones, smart watches, cameras, gaming consoles, and tech accessories are popular for CHRISTMAS (November-December orders), BIRTHDAY (milestone birthdays for teens and adults), and FATHERS_DAY (gadgets and tech tools for dads in May-June).

Confidence boost: electronics + paternal relation + June → FATHERS_DAY. Electronics + November-December → CHRISTMAS.

## Books and Media
Books, e-books, audiobooks, music albums, films, and games are generic gift choices that suggest BIRTHDAY or CHRISTMAS with moderate confidence. Without strong date or message signals, default to UNKNOWN.

## Spa and Wellness
Spa vouchers, bath sets, skincare kits, candles, and wellness products are strongly associated with MOTHERS_DAY (pampering gifts), BIRTHDAY (self-care gifts for friends and partners), VALENTINES_DAY (romantic bath and body sets), and CHRISTMAS (festive gift sets).

Confidence boost: spa or wellness product + maternal relation + April-May → MOTHERS_DAY.

## Food Hampers and Drink
Gourmet hampers, wine, spirits, champagne, and luxury food sets are associated with CHRISTMAS (premium festive hampers), THANKSGIVING (harvest food baskets in November), ANNIVERSARY (fine wine and champagne for couples), and BIRTHDAY (luxury food gifts).

Confidence boost: food hamper + November → THANKSGIVING. Champagne + partner relation → ANNIVERSARY.

## Toys and Games
Toys, board games, puzzles, building sets, video games, and children's gifts are most strongly associated with CHRISTMAS (primary category at Christmas) and BIRTHDAY (gifts for children and gaming enthusiasts). Without date or message signals, default to BIRTHDAY.

Confidence boost: toy product + November-December → CHRISTMAS. Toy + child relation → BIRTHDAY.

## Home and Living
Home decor, kitchenware, cushions, throws, candles, and household items are associated with CHRISTMAS (seasonal decorations and gifts), ANNIVERSARY (gifts for a couple's shared home), and MOTHERS_DAY (practical and decorative home gifts).

## Sporting Goods and Outdoors
Fitness equipment, sports accessories, outdoor gear, and hobby items are most commonly associated with FATHERS_DAY (active gifts for dads), BIRTHDAY (hobby-aligned gifts), and CHRISTMAS (active lifestyle presents).

Confidence boost: sporting goods + paternal relation + June → FATHERS_DAY.
